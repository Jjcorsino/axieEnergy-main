Enemy Energy Calculator and cards(NEW!)

Enemy Energy/Card Calculator is a tool you can use to track how much energy and cards your enemy has for their next round with just a few clicks or Hotkeys.

How it works

You can see we have four buttons(Six for the new version): -1 ENE, +1 ENE, NEXT ROUND and RESET.// -1 CARD +1 CARD
  • -1 ENE: subtracts 1 energy point from the available energy.
  • +1 ENE: add 1 energy point to the available energy.
  • -1 CARD: subtracts 1 card of the enemy's hand.
  • +1 CARD: add 1 card to the enemy's hand.
  • NEXT ROUND: acts as the game round has finished and adds 2 energy points to the available energy and 3 cards to the enemy hand.
  • NEW GAME: acts as a reset button for a new arena match.


Cards and Parts Table

This table can be used to keep track which cards/axie parts' appeared clicking checkboxes.

How it works

Each time you start a new game, you are dealt six random cards from your 24-card deck. When the round begins you should mark on the table which were the cards that you touched or which your enemy uses if you want to use it more aggressively. The columns and rows will eventually fill up. When this happens it means that you ran out of cards in your deck and the game is going to take care of mixing them up and dealing you new cards. To clear the board you should click on the CLEAR button and re-mark the cards/parts that appear.
